About Steem-Engine

Steem-Engine is a platform that enables communities, projects owners, fundraisers, developers as well businesses to create tokens and smart contracts on the steem blockchain. It also has a decentralized marketplace for steem-based tokens. Access to steem-engine requires a steem account. Currently, the MySMT token is 'live' on steem-engine. It will move to the upcoming SMT protocol, developed by Steemit .Inc., when it goes live.
